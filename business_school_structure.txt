This file contains details on the algorithm to scrape Business School classes, for all 11 majors in the business school

1) All majors have BBA Requirements that need to be scraped amoung all majors

2) All 11 majors have specific requirements that need to be scraped too

BBA requirements are quire complicated, so we will start with scraping the specific major requirements by identifying common structures, then moving on to the BBA requirements

Also, note that the BBA requirements has different rules depending on how many majors you're taking

Part 1: Specific Major requirements

Each major has different requirements, but there are 3 main components to each major:

a) Required classes to complete (represent as category with only 1 course)
b) Choose some classes from a list of major classes, limited length
c) Electives - choose from a LARGE variety of possible options
